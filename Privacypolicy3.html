6. Opt-Out Of Personalized Advertising

Opt-out of targeted advertising

You can opt-out of personalized advertising on mobile applications by checking the privacy settings of your Android or iOS device.

Apple devices:

For iOS 14 and iOS 15:

  1. Open Settings

  2. Select Privacy

  3. Select Tracking and disable “Allow Apps to Request to Track”

For iOS 13 and older:

  1. Open Settings

  2. Select Privacy

  3. Select Advertising and enable "Limit Ad Tracking"

 

Android devices:

  1. Open Settings

  2. Select Google

  3. Select Ads and enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

Please note that opting out does not mean that you will stop seeing advertisements in our Services, rather third-party advertising networks will show you contextual advertisements not tailored to your interests. Please, be aware that such actions may result in decrease of advertising quality and less enjoyable user experience.
